01 — THE PROBLEM

Tourism here still runs 30 years behind —
a domestic industry on WhatsApp, cash and paper.

  • No software. Posadas manage reservations in notebooks and WhatsApp threads — no booking system, no connection to Booking.com or Airbnb.
  • No online cohesion. The only way to find most operators is Instagram — scattered, unverified, often offline, with little to no presence on the OTAs travelers actually use.
  • No data, no credit. Banks can’t underwrite what they can’t measure — operators have no verifiable financial records, so upgrade capital doesn’t flow.
  • No training. Hospitality standards sit far below what international travelers now expect — there is no system to certify or level up the workforce.
